Latitude vs. X

The Latitude Project seeks to understand the relationship between various weather attributes and the latitudes where they were measured. The scope of attributes include temparature, humidty, cloudiness, and wind speed.

Data is sourced from the OpenWeather API and includes data from over 500 cities reoughtly evenly distributed accross longitudes and latitudes. Uneven human population densities cause samples to be skewed to the Nothern Hemisphere where population densities, and therefore weather station densities are higher.
